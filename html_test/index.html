Dance History

In dance history Ballet is on eof the oldest types of dances that is also the base for every other type of dance.All dancers must start in ballet training to help them keep good techinque

The 15th-century Italian Renaissance courts are where ballet first appeared. Noblemen and ladies enjoyed opulent occasions, particularly wedding festivities where dancing and music produced a spectacular sight. The steps were taught to the nobles by dance teachers, and the court watched the performances.
