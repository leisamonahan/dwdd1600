Tour of Egypt

A 7 day tour of Cairo and includes a 3 day Nile River Cruise
Cost: $4000 per person
Hotel stays are included in the price with the exception of any nights before or after the 7 day tour.
Recommended Hotel for Cairo
Car Rental (optional)

Day 1 Arrival in Cairo
Welcome to the Land of Pharaohs! At Cairo International Airport, one of our representatives will assist you in customs and deliver you to your driver for the private transfer to your hotel. Check into your room and relax for the rest of the day.
Day 2 Cairo
Start the tour today from the most iconic pyramids complex at Giza, including the Great Pyramid of Giza (also known as the Pyramid of Khufu), the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid of Menkaure. Adjacent to them, you will not meet the mysterious Sphinx.
Spend the afternoon mainly at the Egyptian Museum to admire some treasures from the myriad of antiques from ancient Egypt. Later on, spare some time to explore the Khan El Khalili Market for an insight into local life.
Day 3 Nile River Cruise
Board your Nile cruise in time for lunch and some refreshment. Late in the afternoon, take an Egyptian Felucca sailing on the Nile, sitting back to enjoy the sunset, or stepping ashore the Botanical Gardens on the Kitchener Island.
Day 4 Nile River Cruise
Continue the cruise with ashore tours to the Temple of Kom Ombo and the Temple of Edfu. The former is unique for having the southern half dedicated to the crocodile god Sobek and the northern half dedicated to the falcon god Haroeris. The latter is particularly famous for the inscribed texts and pictures on the building facade providing details of its construction and some mythical stories.
Day 5 Nile River Cruise
Set an early morning sail to Luxor and visit some of the most incredible sites in the country. The Valley of the Kings is the first stop where you will have the chance to explore three tombs of the ancient Pharaohs, followed by the mortuary temple of Queen Hatshepsut - the second female pharaoh in confirmed history. The tour also includes the Colossi of Memnon, which are all that remains of the mortuary temple of Amenhotep III.
Day 6 Luxor
Your Nile cruise comes to an end today. Disembark after breakfast, and then follow your guide to explore the east bank of the Nile, including the Karnak Temple and the Temple of Luxor, which are joined by the two-kilometer-long Avenue of Sphinx.
Day 7 Departure
Time to say goodbye! Enjoy some leisure time before meeting your driver for the transfer to Cairo International Airport.
